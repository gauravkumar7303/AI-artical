AI is Dangerous or Friendly
For Human?

AI(Artificial Intelligence)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) simulates human intelligence in computer systems, enabling them to perform tasks such as learning, reasoning, and self-correction. It includes applications like speech recognition, image processing, and decision-making. AI aims to replicate and enhance human cognitive functions.

Human

A human is a highly intelligent being capable of complex thought, learning, and emotional expression. Humans possess the ability to reason, make decisions, and adapt to new situations. They engage in intricate social interactions and create advanced technologies and cultures.

AI vs Human

The relationship between AI and humans is a complex mix of potential benefits and risks. AI can greatly enhance human capabilities, making tasks easier and more efficient. However, there are concerns about job displacement, ethical dilemmas, and the potential for AI to act unpredictably. Ensuring AI is developed and used responsibly is crucial to maintaining a beneficial and safe coexistence.
